About Our Troop

🌟 Our Story

Troop 43G was chartered in 2024 as an all-girls troop dedicated to the principles of Scouting America. Located in the heart of horse country in Versailles, Kentucky, we embody the spirit of our patrol name - the Mighty Thoroughbreds.

Our troop is built on the foundation of developing confident, capable, and caring young women who are prepared to make a positive impact in their communities and beyond. We believe in adventure, leadership, and friendship.

🎯 Our Mission

To provide girls with opportunities to develop leadership skills, build character, participate in outdoor adventures, and serve their community while having fun and making lasting friendships.

What We Do

🏕️Outdoor Adventures

Camping, hiking, and exploring Kentucky's beautiful outdoors. Learn wilderness survival skills and develop a love for nature.

🎖️Merit Badges

Earn merit badges in subjects ranging from STEM and arts to outdoor skills and community service. Discover new interests and develop expertise.

👥Leadership Development

Take on leadership roles within the troop, plan activities, and develop the confidence to lead in any situation.

❤️Community Service

Make a difference in Versailles and beyond through service projects that help our community and environment.

🐴Local Heritage

Explore our Kentucky heritage with visits to horse farms, historical sites, and local businesses that make our community special.

🎨Skills & STEM

Learn practical life skills, creative arts, technology, and hands-on projects that build confidence and capability.
